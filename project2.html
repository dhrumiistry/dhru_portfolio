Customer Segmentation & Targeting Strategy

This project focused on segmenting customers based on purchasing behavior using Python and machine learning techniques.

The goal was to identify distinct customer groups and recommend targeted marketing strategies for each segment.

Clustering algorithms such as K-Means and DBSCAN were applied to uncover patterns in transaction data.

Insights were visualized using Seaborn and Matplotlib, and a final report outlines the methodology and recommendations.

Tools Used

  • Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn)
  • Jupyter Notebook (analysis & modeling)
  • Matplotlib & Seaborn (visualisations)
  • R-Studio (visualisations)

Key Insights

  • Three distinct customer clusters were identified based on frequency and spending.
  • High-value customers showed strong loyalty and responded well to personalized offers.
  • Low-frequency shoppers preferred discounts and seasonal promotions.
