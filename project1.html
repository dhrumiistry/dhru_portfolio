Exploratory Analysis & Insights

This project involved analyzing a supermarket dataset using Excel, SQL, and Tableau. The goal was to understand customer purchasing behavior and the relationship between advertising and sales.

Tools Used

  • Excel (data cleaning & pivot tables)
  • SQL (queries & joins)
  • Tableau (dashboards & visualizations)

Key Insights

  • ME has highest average income compared to India with the lowest.
  • The top 3 most popular category sold is the same across all countries.
  • Higher education leads to more stable, progressive income.
