Exploratory Analysis & Insights

This project involved analyzing a supermarket dataset using Excel, SQL, and Tableau.

The goal was to understand customer purchasing behavior and the relationship between advertising and sales.

The first section speaks about the key insights gathered from the Tableau dashboard (see screenshot below).

This is followed by the full report with methodology, walking through stages in Excel, SQL & Tableau.

All stages are accompanied by screenshots in the appendix from each application to show the process/code used.

Tools Used

  • Excel (data cleaning & pivot tables)
  • SQL (queries & joins)
  • Tableau (dashboards & visualizations)

Key Insights

  • ME has the highest average income compared to India with the lowest.
  • The top 3 most popular categories sold are consistent across all countries.
  • Higher education leads to more stable, progressive income.
